About

Building at the intersection of AI, Web3, and African innovation

I'm Adeleke Isaac (Xi-kki), a builder creating at the frontier of AI agents, blockchain protocols, and foodtech tools — with Africa as both my starting point and my focus market.

My work spans the full stack: from smart contracts in Solidity and Move, to AI-powered bots and job engines, to content that helps founders navigate AI and Web3. I believe the next wave of innovation will come from combining AI agency with onchain trust — and I'm building the primitives to make that happen.

I've shipped across 0G (VERIDD), Sui (Floe), Telegram (@Paiye_Bot), and Substack (The Xikki Brief). My projects are driven by a single question: how can I build something useful today that compounds into infrastructure tomorrow?
